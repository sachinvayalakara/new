About Company Page


Care was founded by Sachin, who is auto enthusiasts and experts, to provide services for multi-brand cars under one roof. Care services reach out to most vehicles driving in to its facilities, be it an entry level car.


Care claims that the Care franchise network will offer quick maintenance services cost effectively with quick turn-around times. Each facility shall have diagnostic and repair equipment along with trained manpower. The first outlet of Care was launched recently in Kerala, Calicut.
